The Goal

The goal of this game is to eat more pellets than your opponents, without dying!

Print N, W, E or S to move in the given direction. Moving outside the map or onto yourself/opponents will kill you.

  Rules

Your program receives inputs through the standard I/O using the Console.
The unit work as follows:
  • It moves 1 cell based on the Console output you provide during 1 round of the WHILE loop.
  • You are given a list of coordinates (separated with ','). Where the head is the first piece, which will move in the direction you choose.
  • You are given a list of food coordinates, which your snake head can pick up during its movement. Moving onto food will stop your snake and move the head to the tile of the food. (increasing the size by 1)
You lose if:
  • You collide with the borders or your snake head collides with other snakes.
  • You do not supply a valid sequence of actions.

  Expert Rules

Food is eating in place and snake grows instantly onto the tile of the food for simplicity.

  Game Input

The program must first read the initialization data from standard input.

One line: width height playercount foodcount myid


  • width and height: dimensions of the map.
  • playercount: amount of players at the start of the game (2, 3 or 4).
  • foodcount: number of food pellets always available on the map.
  • myid: id of your Snake.
Input during the while loop, to be read before outputting your command:

Line 1: One integer aliveplayercount. Count of alive players

aliveplayercount lines: id score size snake

foodcount lines: x y

  • aliveplayercount: number of alive players
  • id: id of the current snake. Use this ID to find your own snake.
  • score: the points of this player. Highest number of points wins the game.
  • size: length of the next snake variable. Which will be: (size*4-1)
  • snake: number on the format: "5,5,6,5" which is "x0,y0,x1,y1". Indicating the cells occupied by the snake.
  • x and y: position of food spawned around the map.
Output
A single line containing N, W, S or E.
Any text behind this direction is used as a message
Constraints
Time on the first round is 1 sec.
Time on every other round is 50 ms.
