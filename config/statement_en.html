The Goal

The goal of this game is to eat all the food, forever (or 600 frames and max 25000 moves), without dying!

Print N, W, E or S to move in the given direction. Moving outside the map or onto yourself will kill you.
Printing multiple coordinates each round results in multiple movements ex: NNWEEEEES.

  Rules

Your program receives inputs through the standard I/O using the Console.
The unit work as follows:
  • It moves 1 cell based on the Console output you provide during 1 round of the WHILE loop.
  • You are given a list of coordinates. The head is the first piece, which will move in the direction you choose.
  • You are given a list of food coordinates, which your snake head can pick up during its movement. Moving onto food will stop your snake and move the head to the tile of the food. (increasing the size by 1)
You lose if:
  • You collide with the borders or your snake head collides yourself.
  • You do not supply a valid sequence of actions.

  Expert Rules

Food is eaten in place and snake grows instantly onto the tile of the food.
Total score is foods eaten. If you fill the board you get: "foods + 25000 - steps" - to make doing it faster better.
The game uses a pseudo-random number generator to spawn more food, please refer to github for details.

  Game Input

The program must first read the initialization data from standard input.

One line: width height


  • width and height: dimensions of the map.
Input during the while loop, to be read before outputting your command:

seed score snakeSize currentFoundCount

  • seed: current seed of the game. Used to simulate further turns
  • score: the points of the player.
  • snakeSize: number of points in the next snake variable. The snake is given i order
  • currentFoundCount: amount of food present on the board

snakeSize lines: snakeX snakeY Head is the first item in the collection.

  • snakeX: X position of the current snake part
  • snakeY: Y position of the current snake part

currentFoundCount lines: x y

  • x and y: position of food spawned around the map.
Output
A single line containing N, W, S or E.
Include multiple directions to move many times a step. The game will NOT display all moves separately to save server storage.
Constraints
Time on the first round is 1 sec.
Time on every other round is 50 ms.
The view will not render anymore when the number of steps reaches 1000. And it will not render all steps with multiple steps.
